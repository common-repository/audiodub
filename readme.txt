Add Audiodub to your Wordpress Pages with one plugin.

== Description ==

Converting your site into instant audio has never been easier. Add Audiodub to your site with this plugin. 
Simply install this plugin, and add the `[audiodub]` shortcode to any page on your site.
Audiodub has 20+ languages available and 100+ of accents to keep your content unique.

Watch a [1 minute YouTube video](https://youtu.be/3yCRpg4Wc44) to see how it works.

For help or suggestions, reach out to [us](mailto:contact+wordpress@audiodub.app).

= Steps =

1. Install the Audiodub plugin. Follow `plugin` -> `Add new plugin` -> Search for `Audiodub`
2. Navigate to any page where you want audio. Add the shortcode `[audiodub]`. 

That's it! Once you update your page, you'll see your new player within 30 seconds.

Check out [audiodub.app](https://www.audiodub.app/learn) to find out more.
